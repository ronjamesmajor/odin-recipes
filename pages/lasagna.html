Lasagna Recipe

Description:

a nice recipe for amazing full home-made lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Parmesan Cheese.
  • Emental Cheese (Optional).
  • Dry Mozzarella Cheese.
  • Fresh Bolognese Sauce / about 1 litre of Store bought sauce.
  • Béchamel Sauce / or Ricotta Lasagna filling.
  • Lasagna Pasta Sheets.

Steps

  1. Prepare the Bolognese or Jarred pasta sauce.
  2. When sauce is almost done cooking prepare your béchamel or ricotta filling.
  3. If necessary, boil the lasagna noodles following the instructions on the packaging, and then start pre-heating your oven to 175°C/350°F
  4. Spread a thin layer of pasta sauce on the bottom of a large, deep baking tray.
  5. Cover with lasagna sheets and spread a layer of béchamel sauce or ricota, cover with lasagna sheets and spread a layer of pasta sauce. If you want you can finely grate a little bit of parmesan n each layer of bolognese.
  6. Rinse and repeat the previous step until the tray is nearly full or you are running low on béchamel.
  7. Make the last layer béchamel. Grate some parmesan on top of your final layer and cover with dry mozzarella cheese, wrap the top in aluminium foil.
  8. Place the tray in the centre of your pre-heated oven and let it cook for 30-35 minutes.
  9. Once finished, rest for 10 minutes before serving.
